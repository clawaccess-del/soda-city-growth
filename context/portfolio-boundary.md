# Portfolio boundary

This file defines the scope of the Soda City Growth market-site framework.

## Applies to

- Soda City Growth
- future market-focused local marketing websites built from this same internal SOP/framework

Confirmed next-wave targets:

- Albany
- Charleston
- Greensboro
- Greenville
- Richmond
- Rochester
- Syracuse
- Virginia Beach

## Does not apply to

- unrelated websites in other project folders
- non-marketing-site projects
- other brands or website lines unless explicitly adopted later

## Rule

These market-focused marketing websites may share the same internal build framework and service model, but they are separate public entities.

That means:

- no cross-linking between sibling market websites
- no public references between sibling market websites
- no mixed notes, assets, or research across different market-site folders
- no assumption that this rule extends to other unrelated project lines
