Because the click is the expensive part. If the page is weak, the campaign gets more costly and less useful almost immediately.

Sometimes, yes. But performance is usually better when the landing pages are stronger and more aligned with the ad promise.

Does paid traffic help SEO?

Not directly, but strong pages can support both. When the page quality improves, both channels usually get easier to run well.

Who fits this best?

Usually local service businesses with a clear offer, clear lead value, and a need for a better page experience before scaling traffic.
